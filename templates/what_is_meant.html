WHAT IS MEANT BY "HEALTHY RELATIONSHIP"?

In relation to Human Sexuality Education

"Healthy relationship" means one in which both people feel a healthy sense of "self". Each person feels comfortable and safe when spending time with the other person. Two individuals try to meet each other's needs, and each can ask for help and support, within and outside of the relationship without fear of criticism or harm;


How the OAR language reflects our practice:

We asked SHWG members whether the OAR definition reflects their practice. The followingbreaks down the OAR language to incorporate the feedback we received.

1) Both people feel a healthy sense of "self"

    a) This includes the ability to be one's authentic self.

2) Each person feels comfortable and safe when spending time with the other person.

    a) This includes the "CERTS" values of equality, mutual respect, trust,

Two individuals try to meet each other's needs

Each can ask for help and support within and outside of the relationship without fear of criticism or harm.

    a) We take this to mean without fear of undue criticism, shaming, or belittling.
    b) Positively framed, we take this to include open honest communication, and to include both pursuing and ending a relationship in a healthy way.
